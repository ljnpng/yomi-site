Yomi Support

AI-powered RSS reader with audio briefings

Getting Started

Yomi turns your RSS feeds into audio briefings powered by AI. To get started:

  • Add RSS feed URLs in the app
  • Configure your LLM API endpoint and TTS settings in Settings
  • Yomi will automatically fetch, summarize, and generate audio for your feeds

Common Questions

Why do I need to configure an API endpoint?
Yomi uses an OpenAI-compatible LLM API to generate summaries and audio scripts. You provide your own API endpoint and key in Settings. This keeps your data under your control.

What voices are available?
Yomi uses Microsoft Edge TTS, which supports a wide range of voices across multiple languages including English, Chinese, and Japanese.

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Yomi has no servers and does not sync your data to any cloud service.
